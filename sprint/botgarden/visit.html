Admission

Entry to the Greenhouses is free for everyone.

Location

The Botanical Garden is situated in the heart of Aarhus, only a 10-minute walk from the Cathedral.


By bus: Routes 2A, 3A and 5A all stop along the Botanical Garden. You can find alternative routes and timetables at www.midttrafik.dk.


By car: Free parking available for cars and busses in the midst of the Botanical Garden via Peter Holms Vej. Remember to set the parking disc.


By foot: It is possible to access the garden by several different footpaths from the city and Ringgaden.

The Museum shop

The Greenhouses' museum shop is for those who want different things with a botanical twist. You can also check out our online store.

Lunch area

You can enjoy your packed lunch in the picnic area on the 1st floor or on the benches in the patio. You can also buy your lunch in our café which serves traditional food with a twist and enjoy it in the cosy surroundings (requires corona passport).

Lockers

You can keep your coats for free behind the café. Self-service lockers can be found next to the cloakroom (10 DKK).
