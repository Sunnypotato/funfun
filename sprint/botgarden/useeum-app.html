Useeum

With the plant guide, visitors can learn more about the plants in the houses. You can read about the biology of the plants, find out what is blooming right now and see where you can find the individual plants in the houses.


With the plant guide in hand, you can also go on guided tours in the Greenhouses. A biologist or a gardener can show you around and tell exciting stories about the plants.
