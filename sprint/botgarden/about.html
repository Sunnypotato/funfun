Our guidelines

Remember that you are a guest in the Greenhouses, and that it must be a nice place for all visitors to be. So pay attention to the other guests, do not pick the plants and follow the instructions of the staff in general.


If you need to take pictures for school assignments, contact us at least 14 days in advance on 8715 5415 to find out if it is possible.

Establishment

The greenhouses in the Botanical Garden were built in the period 1968-71, and the original snail-shaped building in Victorian style was designed by architect CF Møllers Tegnestue.


The Science Museums are headed by director Bent Lorenzen . The Steno Museum, Ole Rømer Observatory and the Botanical Garden offer dissemination and activities to the public, while the Herbarium (not open to the public) is a research-based unit with 750,000 sheets of dried plants for use in international botany research.
