Experience 4 different climates

The Greenhouses let you explore four different climate zones – each zone featuring beautiful flowers, fascinating plants and stories of nature’s wealth and influence on our daily lives. The greenhouses contain almost 2000 plant species from all over the world, planted in nature-like arrangements and arranged by geography.


Travel around the world in the middle of Aarhus C - start in the Mediterranean climate with the fragrant herbs, move into the dry desert, the humid mountain forests and and end the journey in the tropical rainforest.

Biodiversity

In the exhibition "Wild Growth" we come close to the diversity and distinctive beauty that is found in quite ordinary plants in Danish nature. Afterwards, you can explore the plants in the Greenhouses, perhaps with new inspiration from the exhibition.


When you study a plant or flower up close, a fascinating world reveals itself. A new exhibition in the Greenhouses gives a unique look into the flowers' secret life. All the photos are of ordinary Danish plants.

The Science Museums

The Science Museums belong to Aarhus University and consist of the Steno Museum and the Herbarium in the University Park, the Ole Rømer Observatory in Højbjerg and the Botanical Garden.


The purpose of the Science Museums is to disseminate the natural and medical sciences as well as the latest scientific research results, primarily from Aarhus University.

The USEEUM app

Remember to bring your mobile phone with you when you visit the Greenhouses. You can download the Greenhouses' plant guide USEEUM with stories about plants from the houses.
